## First 10 Minutes

1. Open the [Master Index](MASTER_INDEX.md).
2. Read the [Statutory Compliance Matrix](18-system-mapping/statutory-compliance-matrix.md).
3. Use the [FM Operational Template Library](16-templates/fm-operational-template-library.md) for working records.
4. Use the [FM Audit Checklist Library](17-audit-checklists/fm-audit-checklist-library.md) for assurance checks.
5. Use the [Evidence Retention Policy](18-system-mapping/evidence-retention-policy.md) before deleting or archiving records.
