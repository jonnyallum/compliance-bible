# FM Operational Template Library

## Plain English Summary

This library contains 50 production-ready Facilities Management templates for compliance, contractor control, safety, inspections, evidence, audit and operational escalation.

Each template is designed to be copied into FM Control Hub, Compliance Hub, a site logbook, an audit pack or a board report. The fields are deliberately practical so the template captures action, evidence, ownership and review.

## Template Use Rules

- Do not use a template as a substitute for competent advice where technical judgement is required.
- Complete every owner, date, location and evidence field.
- Attach photographs, reports, certificates or contractor evidence where relevant.
- Escalate high-risk findings immediately.
- Keep completed templates in the evidence library against the site, asset, contractor or incident.
