## Plain English Summary

HSG274 is HSE technical guidance that supports ACOP L8. It gives practical guidance for controlling Legionella risk in water systems.

For FM, HSG274 helps turn the legal duty into operational tasks: temperature monitoring, flushing, cleaning, inspection, record keeping and remedial action.

## Why It Matters in Facilities Management

ACOP L8 explains the duty. HSG274 helps shape the day-to-day control regime. A Facilities Manager should not simply file a water risk assessment and forget it. The control scheme must be converted into tasks, reminders, evidence and escalation.

## Main HSG274 Areas

HSG274 is split across technical topics including:

- evaporative cooling systems;
- hot and cold water systems;
- other risk systems.

For most FM sites, hot and cold water systems are the daily operational focus.

## FM Actions

A Facilities Manager should use the risk assessment and HSG274-style control approach to manage:

- hot water storage and distribution;
- cold water storage and distribution;
- sentinel outlet temperature checks;
- representative outlet checks;
- calorifier inspections where applicable;
- cold water storage tank inspections where applicable;
- low-use outlet flushing;
- shower head cleaning and descaling;
- remedial works after control failures;
- contractor reports and recommendations.

## Evidence to Keep

- Written scheme of control.
- Asset and outlet list.
- Schematic drawings where available.
- Temperature monitoring logs.
- Flushing logs.
- Shower cleaning records.
- Tank inspection records.
- Calorifier records.
- Sampling results where sampling is part of the scheme.
- Remedial action evidence.
- Contractor service reports.

## Review Frequency

Frequency must follow the risk assessment and written scheme. The system should be reviewed when:

- there are changes to the water system;
- occupancy or usage changes;
- monitoring shows loss of control;
- there is a suspected or confirmed case;
- the risk assessment is no longer valid;
- significant remedial works are completed.

## Red Flags

- HSG274 tasks not converted into calendar reminders.
- Monitoring completed but not reviewed.
- Out-of-range temperatures ignored.
- Low-use outlets unknown.
- No schematic or asset list for complex systems.
- Contractor recommendations not tracked.
- No evidence of remedial action closure.
