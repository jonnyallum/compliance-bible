## Purpose

Use this repo to ground Longleat Facilities Management interview answers in real UK compliance, operational controls and board-ready evidence.

## Best Prep Route

1. Read [Risk assessment framework](10-risk-assessments/risk-assessment-framework.md).
2. Read [Contractor management framework](11-contractor-management/contractor-management-framework.md).
3. Read [Permit-to-work framework](12-permit-to-work/permit-to-work-framework.md).
4. Review [Statutory compliance matrix](18-system-mapping/statutory-compliance-matrix.md).
5. Learn evidence language from [Evidence retention policy](18-system-mapping/evidence-retention-policy.md).
6. Practise answers using the `iwfm-level-4` Longleat question and scenario banks.

## Answer Pattern

Use this structure:

1. State the risk in plain English.
2. Say who is affected: visitors, staff, contractors, animals, tenants or the board.
3. Apply immediate controls.
4. Name the competent person or contractor needed.
5. Record evidence.
6. Assign owner and due date.
7. Escalate if risk, budget or authority requires it.
8. Review to prevent recurrence.

## High-Value Longleat Themes

- Power outage with visitors onsite.
- Fire alarm panel fault.
- Suspected asbestos during works.
- Missing water hygiene records.
- Animal enclosure infrastructure defect.
- Maintenance backlog with reduced budget.
- Contractor control in live public environments.
- Board-level compliance reporting.
